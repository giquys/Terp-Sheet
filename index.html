Myrcene
earthy · musky · mango
sedating couch-lock muscle relax pain relief
385→430°
✓ fully active
Most abundant terp across most flower. Believed to increase THC permeability through the blood-brain barrier — intensifies and speeds onset. bp ~334°F — well below your floor of 385°, so you're getting full extraction from the first seconds of your dab. Dominant in most indicas and heavy hybrids.
Myrcene + Linalool
The sleep stack. Linalool's anxiolytic properties soften myrcene's sedation into something cleaner — less heavy-body, more genuine drowsiness. Best for insomnia or high-anxiety nights.
Myrcene + Caryophyllene
Deep body relief. Myrcene handles muscle tension, caryophyllene handles inflammation via CB2. Together they cover most pain profiles without needing a heavily intoxicating amount.
Myrcene + Pinene
Pinene may counteract myrcene-driven memory fog. Gives a more lucid, functional high even when myrcene is dominant — good if you want body relaxation without feeling dumb.
Limonene
citrus · lemon · orange peel
mood lift euphoria anti-anxiety stress relief
385→430°
~ partial
bp ~349°F — already past its boiling point at 385°, so it's vaporizing fast at the start of your dab and largely depleted by the time you hit 430°. You're still getting it, but front-load your inhale to capture the most. One of the best for anxiety-prone sessions — tends to ease rather than worsen paranoia.
⚡Inhale hard in the first 20–25 sec. Most of your limonene is gone before you hit peak temp — waiting costs you the best of it.
Limonene + Linalool
Anxiety-kill stack. Limonene provides mood elevation, linalool grounds the edges. Together they're reliable for social settings or when you want high-THC without the ceiling fan of paranoia.
Limonene + Pinene
Peak daytime. Dual uplift on mood and focus — limonene handles the emotional brightness, pinene sharpens cognitive function. Good for creative work or long dog walks.
Limonene + Caryophyllene
Balanced hybrid effect. Caryophyllene adds mild body weight and stress relief to limonene's lift. Popular combo in well-reviewed hybrids for a reason.
Caryophyllene
black pepper · spice · diesel
anti-inflammatory pain relief stress relief CB2 agonist
385→430°
✓ fully active
bp ~399°F — hits right in the middle of your range. It's vaporizing at peak efficiency through most of your 90-second cycle. The only terpene that binds directly to CB2 receptors, making it genuinely anti-inflammatory. Holds up very well when dabbing and delivers consistent body relief.
Caryophyllene + Myrcene
Full-spectrum pain relief. Myrcene relaxes muscle tissue, caryophyllene handles underlying inflammation. Redundant mechanisms covering different receptor types — better together than either alone.
Caryophyllene + Humulene
Anti-inflammatory double-down. Both have strong anti-inflammatory activity and they co-occur naturally in many strains. Good for post-workout or joint soreness without a heavy high.
Caryophyllene + Limonene
Mood + body without sedation. Limonene keeps things bright, caryophyllene handles stress at the body level. One of the most functional combos for evening use before bed but not ready to sleep.
Pinene
pine · fresh · forest air
alertness memory retention focus bronchodilator
385→430°
↓ mostly lost
bp ~311°F — well below your 385° floor, so it's largely vaporized before your dab really gets going. You're getting trace amounts at best. If pinene's effects matter to you (cognitive clarity, memory retention), flower at lower temps is more effective. Still present in concentrates but your dab range extracts it near-instantly and it's gone.
⚡Don't chase pinene in concentrates at your temps — it's not there. Better off finding a piney live resin and vaping flower for the pinene effect specifically.
Pinene + Myrcene
Lucid body high. Myrcene relaxes, pinene keeps you mentally present. Good when you want to chill without zoning out completely.
Pinene + Limonene
Functional daytime stack. Both are mentally energizing via different mechanisms — limonene on mood, pinene on cognition and memory. One of the cleanest combos for staying productive.
Linalool
lavender · floral · light spice
anxiolytic sedating muscle relax anti-depressant
385→430°
✓ survives
bp ~388°F — right at your starting temp, so it begins vaporizing immediately and stays active through your full 90-second cycle. One of the best-preserved terps in your range. The "lavender" terp. Strong anxiolytic without being crushingly sedating at moderate doses — very synergistic, punches above its % when paired with other calm terps.
⚡Your 385° floor is nearly perfect for linalool. Steady sustained inhale works better than front-loading here — it stays active the whole cycle.
Linalool + Myrcene
The sleep stack — see Myrcene entry. Linalool specifically smooths out myrcene's heavier sedation, targeting anxiety while myrcene handles the body knock.
Linalool + Limonene
Anxiety-kill stack — see Limonene entry. Limonene lifts mood, linalool prevents the mood from tipping into anxious territory. One of the best combos for high-THC concentrates.
Linalool + Caryophyllene
Stress + inflammation double coverage. Linalool handles the neurological stress response, caryophyllene handles the physical. Good for tension headaches or post-stress evenings.
Terpinolene
floral · fresh · herbal · faintly piney
uplifting creative euphoric can spike anxiety
385→430°
~ partial
bp ~369°F — below your floor, so it's already vaporizing fast at 385° and mostly spent before you reach 430°. You get a meaningful hit early in the dab but it dissipates quickly. Heady, cerebral, energetic high — less predictable than limonene for anxiety. Pair with linalool if you're prone to getting racy.
⚡Try dropping to 375–380° if your Switch 2 allows it — you found this yourself and it works. Keeps terpinolene active longer instead of flashing it off immediately. Front-load your inhale hard in the first 30 sec.
Terpinolene + Linalool
The safety net. Linalool's anxiolytic effect blunts terpinolene's edge. Makes terpinolene-dominant strains accessible if you're prone to getting racy — look for both on the label before committing.
Terpinolene + Myrcene
Tempers the head-rush. Myrcene adds body weight that grounds terpinolene's more volatile mental effects. Results in a more balanced, sustained high.
Ocimene
sweet · herbal · tropical · woody
uplifting energizing mood boost
385→430°
↓ gone
bp ~140–170°F — gone entirely before your dab starts. You're getting zero ocimene effect at 385–430°. If it's on a concentrate label, you're just smelling residual aroma. Irrelevant for dabbing — only matters for low-temp flower vaping. Don't factor it into concentrate purchasing decisions.
⚡Skip ocimene entirely when reading concentrate labels. It's not a factor at any reasonable dab temp — it's a flower/vape terp only.
Ocimene + Limonene
Amplified brightness. Both are uplifting via different mechanisms, and their aromatics complement each other strongly. When present together the effect is noticeably more energetic and socially engaging.
Humulene
hoppy · earthy · woody · beer-like
anti-inflammatory appetite suppression subtle calming
385→430°
✓ fully active
bp ~222°F — fully vaporized well before 385°, so it's completely extracted from the first moment of your dab. Same terp that makes IPAs hoppy. Notable for appetite suppression — one of few terps that counteracts munchies. Usually secondary alongside caryophyllene rather than dominant.
Humulene + Caryophyllene
Anti-inflammatory power combo — see Caryophyllene entry. These two co-occur naturally and complement each other's mechanisms. If both are present at >0.2% each, expect meaningful body relief.
